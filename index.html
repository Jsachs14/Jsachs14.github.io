About

Jonah Sachs, previously a researcher at WashU, graduated with a B.S. in Computer Science and Physics in 2025. He specializes in quantum mechanics, technical writing and communication, semiconductor and superconductor physics, nano/microfabrication, and electrical control systems management and development. Jonah just finished up an internship at QuEra Computing Inc, working as a intern on the Quantum Controls/QPU Software team. Jonah has previously conducted research in a wide range of topics within the fields of quantum technologies and semiconductor engineering, and is still actively involved with chemical engineering research. In his spare time, Jonah previously built and managed the electrical and data acquisition systems for an FSAE student-built race car. To fill that void, he still messes with microcontrollers, software engineering, and hobby electronics when there's time. (Health Pending) Jonah loves to play golf, albeit with an ugly hook. Jonah is currently looking for full-time roles, specifically in the Greater Boston area or remote. Please reach out if you think there could be a good fit!
