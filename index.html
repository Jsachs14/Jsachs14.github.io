About

Jonah Sachs, previously a researcher at WashU, graduated with a B.S. in Computer Science and Physics in 2025. He specializes in quantum mechanics, semiconductor and superconductor physics, nano/microfabrication, and electrical control systems management and development. Jonah is currently employed at QuEra Computing Inc, working as a intern on the Quantum Controls team. Jonah has previously conducted research in areas such as quantum computation and semiconductor engineering, and is still involved with research involving chemical engineering. In his spare time, Jonah previously built and managed the electrical and data acquisition systems for an FSAE student-built race car. He plays golf with an ugly hook and a nasty short game, when there's time.
